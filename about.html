About Us

2022 saw the start of Francis Brothers' Racing in the garage of our family home in Whatawhata. Aiden Francis started his breakout season at Meremere Dirt Track Club in November 2022, winning all three class races of the day. He would go on to win three races at Waharoa Speedway, an achievement that we're all extremely proud of. He was also awarded driver of the day at Waiuku Dirt Track Club, an incredible honor that recognizes his skill and dedication to the sport.

Starting his first competitive season in 2023, Bradley Francis spent the 2022/2023 season shadowing his brother Aiden to gain an understanding of the format and timings of the pits on race day.

Aiden and Bradley's Parents Rob and Jan come from a rich background of motor racing. During their twenties they spent most of their time building, fixing, and competitivley racing rally and circuit cars. Rob Francis saw success in the wolfmasters top half series, while Jan was one of the flying females in the SS-2000 circuit racing series. Their support, knowledge, and passion has been a large driving foce behind the success of Francis Brothers Racing.
