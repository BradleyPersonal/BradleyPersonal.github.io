Welcome to Francis Brother's Racing

Raising Awareness for I Am Hope's Gumboot Friday

Raising Awareness for I Am Hope and their efforts to help connect young people with counsellors or therapists. All donations made to the Gumboot Friday Fund directly support kids in need. This is a cause that we're incredibly passionate about and we're grateful for the opportunity to use our platform to raise awareness for such an important issue. We believe that it's our responsibility to use our success in the racing community to give back to those who need it most, and we're proud to be associated with such a great organization.
